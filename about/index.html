Vanni Support

Making navigation accessible for everyone.

How It Works

Vanni is designed to assist visually impaired users by providing audio feedback for the elements shown on the screen. Simply hover your cursor over an element, and the system will read it aloud.

FAQ

What is Vanni?

Vanni is an accessibility feature that provides audio feedback for on-screen elements.

How can I enable the sound navigation?

The sound navigation feature is enabled by default. Simply move your cursor, and you will hear the audio.

What sounds will I hear?

You will hear the names of elements such as buttons, links, and headings as you hover over them.
