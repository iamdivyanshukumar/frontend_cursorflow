For whom?

Dristhi Mouse is ideal for individuals with physical disabilities, including those with limited hand mobility or paralysis. It provides hands-free control using eye movements, making it perfect for users with mobility challenges, elderly individuals, and professionals. Ideal for presentations, gaming, or everyday computing, Dristhi enhances accessibility and offers a seamless, precise experience across all major operating systems.

Features

Augmented pointer

Visual aid with big pointer and intersecting axes to easily identify the position of the pointer.

Up to two switches

Switch control with one or two switches. Compatible with Bluetooth switches and cable switches (3.5 mm connector).

Customizable

Select the menu. Choose the color and size of the pointer. Adjust the timings and scanning speed to your needs.

Easy to install

You can start using Mouse4all Switch in minutes. Follow the instructions in the quick-start guide included in the app.
